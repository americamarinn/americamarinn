Art & Design

América is a Fronteriza from San Diego, California, and the daughter of Mexican immigrants from Veracruz and Tamaulipas, Mexico. Her art delves into themes of gender fluidity and embodiment, reflecting unique non-binary humanoids in layered watercolor and gouache. Through her work, América demonstrates the beauty of fluidity and identity.
