About Me

Hello and welcome! This is my personal website where I share a bit about myself, my work, and my interests. My name is América Marín Reyes, and I am currently a third-year student at Brown University studying Computer Science and (Linguistic) Anthropology BA.

I demonstrate a profound interest in utilizing software and graphic design skills as catalysts for societal transformation. I also engage in rigorous research endeavors pertaining to Latine and Indigenous identity, reflecting my dedication to scholarly exploration.

Art & Design

América is a Fronteriza from San Diego, California, and the daughter of Mexican immigrants from Veracruz and Tamaulipas, Mexico. Her art delves into themes of gender fluidity and embodiment, reflecting unique non-binary humanoids in layered watercolor and gouache. Through her work, América demonstrates the beauty of fluidity and identity.
