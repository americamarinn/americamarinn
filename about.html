About Me

Hello and welcome! This is my personal website where I share a bit about myself, my work, and my interests. My name is América Marín Reyes, and I am currently a third-year student at Brown University studying Computer Science and (Linguistic) Anthropology BA. I demonstrate a profound interest in utilizing software and graphic design skills as catalysts for societal transformation.

I also engage in rigorous research endeavors pertaining to Latine and Indigenous identity, reflecting my dedication to scholarly exploration. Furthermore, I display a passion for linguistic acquisition and its pragmatic application within professional spheres.
